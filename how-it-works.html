METHOD

Discovery → Wiring → Guardrails → Learning loop.

Built for Business Owners, Management roles, and anyone who gets buried under multiple APPs for routing, monitoring, and chasing projects, invoices, and anything that drives their daily work. We remove the chaos by building simple systems inside your existing tools — then keeping them reliable through logs, custom safeguards, and timely improvement.

1
Discovery (measured in days)

We map your chaos to one or two measurable outcomes. We read your queue, watch the handoffs, and identify dependency points where work stops without a person.

Output: A 1-page system plan: what to automate, what to log, what to keep human.
2
Wiring (inside the tools you already use)

We implement routing, categorisation, logging, and follow-ups directly inside your stack: Gmail/Outlook, Sheets/Excel, Slack/Chat, CRMs, project tools.

Output: A working pilot in production — scoped, observable, and reversible.
3
Guardrails (so it stays boringly reliable)

We don't just automate tasks; we automate compliance. Our team ensures that every new implementation follows your company’s specific rules before it ever goes live.

Output: Silent outcomes with : custom processes + control + human checkpoints.
4
Learning loop (improves every week)

We track misroutes, false positives, response times, and bottlenecks. The system improves without re-platforming your ops.

Output: A measurable reliability loop: fewer misses, fewer handoffs, less chaos.
When TellNo1 is a fit
  • • High volume inbox / ops queues
  • • SLAs and customer expectations
  • • Multiple teams / shifts / handoffs
  • • KPIs that require constant checking
When it isn’t
  • • You want a generic SaaS dashboard
  • • You don’t want logs or controls
  • • You prefer “AI magic” over reliability
  • • You need a full team replacement overnight
Typical timeline

Discovery: 2–5 days. Pilot: 2–4 weeks. Iteration: weekly.

We ship small, observable pieces — then expand.
