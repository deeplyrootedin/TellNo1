Case studies

Story snapshots.

Anonymized, founder-to-founder. No fluff — just the problem, what we wired, and what changed.

Shared inbox became a system (not a queue)
Ops / Support
Problem: Managers were manually scanning threads, assigning work, and chasing replies. SLA misses were discovered after the fact.
Wiring: Smart inbox monitoring + categorisation + routing rules + ageing alerts + audit logs. Escalations routed to the right channel automatically.
Outcome: The queue stopped depending on one person. “Needs reply” surfaced instantly. Fewer misses, less chasing, faster handoffs.
Gmail/Outlook Routing SLA monitoring Audit logs
KPI checks moved from humans to guardrailed automation
Operations
Problem: Daily KPI health checks were repetitive and inconsistent. Issues were noticed late, after downstream impact.
Wiring: Scheduled KPI audits + anomaly detection thresholds + auto actions (pause/flag) with human checkpoints + weekly improvement loop.
Outcome: Problems surfaced earlier with evidence. Fewer escalations. Operators focused on decisions, not checking.
KPI health Anomaly flags Guardrails Weekly loop
Follow-ups stopped being “someone’s job”
Finance / RevOps
Problem: Invoices and vendor/client follow-ups were dependent on memory and manual nudges. Aging slipped quietly.
Wiring: Invoice aging rules + reminder cadence + escalation tiers + status logging into Sheets/CRM, all inside existing tools.
Outcome: Aging reduced and visibility increased. The system chased; humans approved edge cases.
Invoice tracking Follow-up agent Escalations Sheets/CRM
Rostering aligned to real workflow load
Workforce / Shifts
Problem: Static rosters created dead zones and burnout. Coverage didn’t match workflow patterns across days.
Wiring: Workload heatmap + coverage rules + guardrails for shift changes + weekly roster recommendations with approvals.
Outcome: Fewer dead zones. Better coverage on heavy periods. Less firefighting on weekends.
